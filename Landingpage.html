About Us

India Tour

India is a land of rich history and heritage. The country boasts of numerous historical places that are of great significance and are a testament to the legacy of ancient civilizations that flourished here. The Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, is a symbol of love and is a major tourist attraction. The Red Fort in Delhi is a symbol of the Mughal Empire and is a major historical monument. The Ajanta and Ellora Caves are famous for their rock-cut sculptures and paintings that depict the Buddhist religion. The Hampi ruins in Karnataka are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit for anyone interested in history.
